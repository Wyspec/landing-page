Project Nebula
From day one, our mission has been to grow a community of friendly people that are compassionate, considerate and fun. The members of Project Nebula have come together to form more than just a Minecraft server. Our community is tight, welcoming, and full of personable, diverse, interesting people! Hope you give us a shot!
More info about our server
We use Purpur for its flexibility in configurability as well as server performance.
Check out our interactive Dynmap, featuring a world border of 7k across, and 12k to the east!
We've worked hard on providing community features, now featuring resettable structures (Monuments, Pillar Outposts, Ancient Cities, and more!)
Nebula is fully committed to creating a space where all players feel welcome and included. All people are welcome to join us!
